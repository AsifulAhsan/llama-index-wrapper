FiNext Lite — SME ERP & Finance by Logiqbits

FiNext Lite is an all-in-one ERP and financial management solution built specifically for small and medium-sized enterprises.

Key Features:
- Financial Management: General ledger, accounts payable, accounts receivable, bank reconciliation, and financial statements.
- Inventory Management: Track stock levels, manage warehouses, set reorder points, and handle purchase orders and goods receipts.
- Sales & Invoicing: Create quotations, sales orders, invoices, and receipts. Track payment status and overdue invoices.
- Purchase Management: Raise purchase orders, manage suppliers, track deliveries, and process vendor invoices.
- Reporting: Profit & loss statements, balance sheets, cash flow reports, and tax summaries.
- Multi-currency Support: Handle transactions in multiple currencies with automatic exchange rate updates.

ShopAP — Accounts Payable Automation:
ShopAP is Logiqbits' accounts payable automation tool integrated within the Core suite.
- Automates invoice capture from emails and scanned documents.
- Three-way matching between purchase orders, goods receipts, and vendor invoices.
- Approval workflows for invoice sign-off before payment.
- Scheduled payment runs with bank integration.

Common Use Cases:
- A finance manager wants to generate a monthly profit and loss report.
- A warehouse manager wants to check current stock levels and trigger a reorder.
- An accountant wants to reconcile bank statements against recorded transactions.
- A business owner wants to see which vendor invoices are overdue for payment.
