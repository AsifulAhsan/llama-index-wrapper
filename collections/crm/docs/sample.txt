SaleSense CRM by Logiqbits

SaleSense CRM is a customer relationship management platform designed to help sales teams track leads, manage pipelines, and close deals faster.

Key Features:
- Lead Management: Capture, assign, and track leads from multiple sources including web forms, emails, and manual entry.
- Sales Pipeline: Visual kanban-style pipeline showing deals at each stage — New, Contacted, Qualified, Proposal, Negotiation, Closed Won, Closed Lost.
- Contact Management: Store and manage customer profiles including contact details, interaction history, notes, and attached documents.
- Activity Tracking: Log calls, meetings, emails, and follow-up tasks against each contact or deal.
- Reports & Analytics: Generate sales performance reports, conversion rates, team leaderboards, and revenue forecasts.
- Team Collaboration: Assign leads to sales reps, set reminders, and leave internal notes on deals.

Common Use Cases:
- A sales manager wants to see which deals are stuck in the pipeline and need follow-up.
- A sales rep wants to log a call with a client and set a follow-up reminder for next week.
- Management wants a monthly revenue forecast based on current pipeline values.
