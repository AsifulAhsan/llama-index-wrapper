Logiqbits HCM — Human Capital Management

Logiqbits HCM is a comprehensive HR management solution covering the full employee lifecycle from onboarding to offboarding.

Key Features:
- Employee Records: Centralized database for all employee information including personal details, job history, contracts, and documents.
- Attendance & Leave: Track daily attendance, manage leave requests, approve or reject leaves, and view leave balances.
- Payroll Management: Automated payroll calculation based on attendance, overtime, deductions, and bonuses. Supports multiple pay grades.
- Performance Reviews: Set KPIs, conduct quarterly or annual reviews, and track employee performance over time.
- Recruitment: Post job openings, manage applicants, schedule interviews, and onboard new hires.
- Org Chart: Visual organizational hierarchy showing departments, teams, and reporting lines.

Common Use Cases:
- An HR manager wants to process monthly payroll for 50 employees automatically.
- An employee wants to apply for annual leave and track its approval status.
- Management wants to review performance scores before deciding on promotions.
- HR wants to onboard a new hire and set up their profile, access, and contracts.

Departments Supported:
HCM supports unlimited departments and sub-departments. Each department can have its own leave policies, working hours, and pay structures.
