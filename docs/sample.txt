Logiqbits is a technology company offering business solutions.

FiNext Lite is an SME ERP and Finance solution that helps small and medium businesses manage their finances, inventory, and operations in one place.

ShopAP is an accounts payable automation tool that streamlines invoice processing and vendor payments.

SaleSense CRM is a customer relationship management platform designed to help sales teams track leads, manage pipelines, and close deals faster.

HCM (Human Capital Management) is Logiqbits' HR solution covering employee management, payroll, attendance, and performance tracking.
